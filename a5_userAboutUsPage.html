Amidst the vibrant streets of a bustling city, nestled within a modest corner, "Long Ding Huo Guo" cast its inviting glow. This humble startup hotpot restaurant, helmed by the passionate chef Mei Lin, embodied a fusion of tradition and innovation. Mei Lin's culinary journey, rooted in her grandmother's teachings, imbued the eatery with a unique charm, promising warmth and flavor to all who ventured through its doors.
