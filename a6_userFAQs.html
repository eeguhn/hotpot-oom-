Q: What is hotpot?
A: Hotpot is a communal dining experience where diners cook a variety of fresh ingredients in a shared pot of simmering broth at the center of the table.
Q: How do I make a reservation?
A: You can make a reservation online through our website or by calling our restaurant directly. Visit the "Reservation" page for more details.
Q: Are there vegetarian options available?
A: Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian ingredients and vegetarian-friendly broths to cater to our vegetarian guests.
Q: Is there parking available at the restaurant?
A: Yes, we have dedicated parking spaces for our customers. Please check our "Contact Us" page for parking details and availability.
Q: Can I bring my own ingredients to the hotpot?
A: We provide a curated selection of fresh ingredients to ensure quality and safety. Outside ingredients are not allowed to maintain our high standards.
Q: Are there options for spice levels in the broths?
A: Absolutely! You can choose from a range of spice levels for the broths, catering to both those who prefer a mild experience and those who enjoy a spicier kick.
Q: Do you accommodate food allergies?
A: Yes, we take allergies seriously. Please inform our staff about any allergies when making a reservation or upon arrival, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.
Q: Can I order takeout for the hotpot experience at home?
A: Yes, we offer takeout options for our hotpot menu. Visit our "Order Now" page on the website to explore the takeout menu and place your order.
Q: Are children welcome at Long Ding Huo Guo?
A: Absolutely! We welcome guests of all ages. We offer a kid-friendly menu and provide a comfortable dining environment for families.
Q: Do you have private dining options for special occasions?
A: Yes, we offer private dining options for special occasions and events. Contact our team for more information on reservations and customized experiences.
